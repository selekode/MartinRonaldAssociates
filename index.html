Our Services

Auditing

We provide thorough and independent audits to ensure accuracy, transparency, and compliance with financial regulations.

Audit & Assurance is a service offered by MRA that provides an independent and objective evaluation of an organisation's financial information, operations, and internal controls.
The objective is to provide assurance that the financial information and internal controls are in compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.
Audit & Assurance services also include helping organizations improve the effectiveness of their operations and internal controls.
An audit is necessary to ensure the credibility of an organization’s financial records and to provide assurance to stakeholders that the financial statements are presented fairly and in accordance with applicable accounting standards.

Tax Consultancy

Optimise your tax strategy with expert advice tailored to your business, ensuring compliance and reducing liability.

Specialized services offered by MRA that offers advice and assistance to clients on financial matters such as strategic planning, financial restructuring, and capital raising. Finance consultants help to analyse and evaluate a company’s financial situation and develop solutions to improve their financial performance.
We can also provide analysis and advice on financial transactions, such as mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures. We also help to develop financial models, review and negotiate financial contracts, and provide assistance with budgeting and forecasting.
Our consultants help in analysing your organisation’s financial situation to develop solutions aimed at mitigating risks and improve performance.

Retirement & Succession Planning

Plan ahead with confidence—secure your legacy and ensure a smooth transition for your business or personal estate.

In succession planning we take a deep dive assessing and auditing the financial governance of and talent in the organisation in order to answer three fundamental questions:
i) Are there enough potential successors available to take on leadership?
ii) Are the financials good enough?
iii)Are there the right skills and competences for the future?
For the individual, we look to support the foundations and address the gaps and wishes of the indivual in context with personal circumsatnces and sensitivities.
