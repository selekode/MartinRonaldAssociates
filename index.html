About Us

MRA is a centrally managed audit firm based in Nairobi, Kenya that shares its resources, technology, methodologies, strength and insights to serve those in need of audit, accounting, and financial consultancies. We are structured in a way that gives you quicker access to our senior professionals who will give your requirements adequate deep thought so that you get timely and decisive advice best suited for you.

Our Mission

To provide the highest level of professional services to meet our client's needs through mutual financial success. To maintain integrity and honesty in everything we do. To provide for our team members' development, growth, and accountability by offering opportunities to achieve personal and professional success.

Our Vision

To simplify accounting and eliminate fear of the subject of money, and to be regionally recognised as the firm of choice for easing access to financial advice.
